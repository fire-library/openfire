Sections 1.2.1 and 1.2.2 of _BR 187 (2014)_ discuss the compartment fire
framework and the applicability and use of the ventilation factor.
 These sections emphasize that many residential compartments will have a
 ventilation factor $O$ above $20$ $m^{-0.5}$, leading to ventilation
 controlled post-flashover fires.

The ventilation factor is commonly used to describe the ventilation conditions
for a compartment fire in fire science research. Its calculation in fire safety
and fire protection engineering usually constitutes an intermediate step towards
describing the conditions inside the compartment.
