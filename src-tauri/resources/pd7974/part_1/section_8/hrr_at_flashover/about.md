Equations 28, 29, and 33 in PD 7974-1 are key for calculating heat release rates in compartment fires. Equation 28, based on Thomas' analysis, estimates the heat release rate required for flashover, assuming a 600°C temperature rise in the upper layer. Equation 29, developed by McCaffrey et al., uses a 500°C temperature rise and includes heat transfer through enclosure boundaries. Equation 33, based on Kawagoe's work, calculates the heat release rate in a ventilation-controlled fire, where the fire is limited by oxygen supply. These equations are critical for fire behavior modeling and fire safety design.
